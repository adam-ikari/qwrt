## FAIL（8 个）— 分类分析

### A. qwrt polyfill 真实缺陷（6 个）

| # | 测试 | 完整测试名 | 断言失败信息 | 根因 |
|---|------|-----------|-------------|------|
| 1 | EventTarget dispatch | "A constructed EventTarget implements dispatch correctly" | `expected null but got object "[object Object]"` | `dispatchEvent()` 返回事件对象，规范要求返回事件未被取消时为 false/对象 |
| 2 | AddEventListener once (nested) | "Once listener should be invoked only once even if the event is nested" | `Once handler should only be invoked once expected 1 but got 2` | 嵌套事件触发时 once listener 被调 2 次 |
| 3 | URL searchParams clear | "URL.searchParams updating, clearing" | `expected "" but got "a=b"` | `searchParams.delete()` 后 toString() 未清空 |
| 4 | URL search↔searchParams 同步 | "URL.searchParams and URL.search setters, update propagation" | `expected "e=f&g=h" but got "a=b&c=d"` | `.search` 赋值后 searchParams 不同步 |
| 5 | TextDecoder streaming EOF | "TextDecoder end-of-queue handling" (×2) | `expected "�" but got "\0"` | stream:true finish() 返回 \0 而非正确替换字符 |
| 6 | TextDecoder UTF-16 decode | "Decode sample: utf-16le/be/utf-16" (×3) | `invalid code point` | UTF-16 编码解码抛出异常 |
